Who We Are


Yoga Studio is a Schaumburg, IL based premier yoga institute. Our workshops, taught by leading yoga experts, provide in-depth yoga immersions as well as the latest in yoga research and techniques. Whether you need to move your body, quiet your mind or connect to your spirit, there is a place for you at Yoga Studio. Whether you are new to yoga or an experienced practitioner, there is a place for you at Yoga Studio. Whether you are injured, overweight, out of shape or in the best shape of your life, there is a place for you at Yoga Studio.


We infuse our yoga classes and teacher training with quality, integrity and a sense of humor. Our highly trained, passionate teachers make yoga accessible to everyone and offer a practice that can be applied on and off the mat.


Our studio specializes in private (1:1) training and provides small group classes in Pilates mat and machine, Yoga, Tabata HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training). Our trainers/instructors are certified professionals who have the highest levels of education, training, and real world experience. We are conveniently located in the heart of Schaumburg, near Schaumburg Road.


Being a small, non-intimidating studio allows us to provide individualized attention which equal greater benefits and gains for your time (& money) than at other exercise facilities. Purchasing private training packages and our class registration and cancellation process is streamlined. All clients have an online web account via our website.
